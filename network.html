GreenFields AgroTech

GreenFields AgroTech was founded as a small local farm-to-table produce company aimed at providing fresh, organic vegetables to urban markets in northern India. Neha, a passionate farmer, and Arun, an agricultural expert, noticed the challenges of sourcing high-quality, pesticide-free produce for urban dwellers who were increasingly concerned about food safety and sustainability.

Techtonic Innovations

TechTonic Innovations was founded as a small IT solutions company, initially focused on creating software for local small businesses. Rohit, a software engineer, and Sneha, an expert in business analytics, recognized that many local businesses in Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities lacked access to advanced software tools and technology to streamline their operations. They began by offering affordable, custom solutions for small retailers and manufacturers.
