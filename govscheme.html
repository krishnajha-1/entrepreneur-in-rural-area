Government Schemes for Small Businesses in India

Explore the various government schemes and programs available to support small businesses.

Startup India

An initiative to boost startups by providing tax exemptions, funding, and more.

Startup India offers tax exemptions for the first three years, easier compliance rules, and funding support for innovative startups.
