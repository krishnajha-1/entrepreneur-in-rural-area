GreenFields AgroTech

Company Description

Founded: 2010 in Hapur, Uttar Pradesh.

Founder(s): Neha Sharma and Arun Kumar.

Initial Focus: GreenFields AgroTech was founded as a small local farm-to-table produce company aimed at providing fresh, organic vegetables to urban markets in northern India. Neha, a passionate farmer, and Arun, an agricultural expert, noticed the challenges of sourcing high-quality, pesticide-free produce for urban dwellers who were increasingly concerned about food safety and sustainability.

Growth & Expansion: Starting with a small farm in Hapur, GreenFields began selling directly to local markets and supermarkets. They focused on producing organic vegetables and fruits and offering farm tours for educational purposes. By 2015, their farm-to-doorstep service began to gain popularity, and they started expanding their network of partner farms across the region.

National Reach: By 2018, the company had expanded to several states, including Punjab, Haryana, and Uttar Pradesh, and began exporting to metro cities like Delhi and Mumbai. Their strong commitment to sustainable farming practices, eco-friendly packaging, and the growing demand for organic food fueled their success. GreenFields became a household name for quality organic produce.

Global Recognition: In 2022, GreenFields AgroTech raised significant funding and expanded globally, exporting organic products to international markets, including Europe and the Middle East. The company introduced its own branded line of organic juices, snacks, and packaged products. GreenFields now stands as a leader in the organic food market, committed to bringing clean, green produce to millions of consumers worldwide.
