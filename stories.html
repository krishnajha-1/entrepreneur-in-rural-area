Story 1: Overcoming Challenges in Rural India

Krishna's journey began in rural Rajasthan, where he faced the challenges of providing basic education resources. He started a low-cost e-learning initiative, quickly gaining recognition in local villages.

In 2012, Raghav launched an online platform to provide affordable educational content to students in rural Rajasthan. Despite challenges like poor internet connectivity, he collaborated with local schools and launched mobile-based learning modules. By 2015, the initiative reached 50 villages, bringing quality education to thousands of children.

Story 2: Innovating in the Food Industry

Adarsh moved to Delhi and saw an opportunity to innovate in the food delivery industry. He introduced a plant-based meal service targeting health-conscious urban professionals.

In 2017, Raghav started a plant-based meal subscription service. With a focus on organic ingredients and eco-friendly packaging, his meals quickly became popular among urban professionals. By 2019, the service expanded to Mumbai and Bengaluru, becoming a pioneer in sustainable food in India.
