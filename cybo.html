Company Description

Founded: 2015 in Indore, Madhya Pradesh.

Founder(s): Rohit Verma and Sneha Gupta.

Initial Focus: TechTonic Innovations was founded as a small IT solutions company, initially focused on creating software for local small businesses. Rohit, a software engineer, and Sneha, an expert in business analytics, recognized that many local businesses in Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities lacked access to advanced software tools and technology to streamline their operations. They began by offering affordable, custom solutions for small retailers and manufacturers.

Growth & Expansion: In its early years, TechTonic developed affordable POS (Point of Sale) systems and inventory management software, which became popular among local businesses in Indore. By 2017, the company had expanded its product offerings to include CRM (Customer Relationship Management) software and cloud-based tools tailored to the needs of small businesses. The company saw rapid growth as it ventured into more cities across India.

National Reach: By 2019, TechTonic Innovations had become a recognized name in the Indian IT industry, offering software solutions to thousands of businesses, including retail chains, e-commerce platforms, and manufacturing companies. The company built a network of clients across India, ranging from local shops to mid-sized businesses in urban areas.
